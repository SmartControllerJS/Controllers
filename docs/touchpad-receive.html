Smartcontroller Tutorial

  1. Scan the code with your phone, a canvas should be displayed on the smarphone screen.
  2. Touch the screen, each finger will appear as a dot on the browser canvas.
  3. Connect with another phone.
    When multiple users are interacting with the screen their fingers will show in different colours.
